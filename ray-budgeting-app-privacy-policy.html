Privacy Policy

Ray Budget: Expense Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("App") handles information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

The App is designed to work offline and does not require user accounts or collect personal financial data.

  • Local Data: All financial data you enter (such as expenses and budgets) is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data.
  • Crash and Diagnostic Data: We may collect limited non-personal diagnostic information through third-party crash reporting services to improve app stability.

This may include device information, app version, and crash logs. No personal financial data is collected or transmitted.

2. How We Use Information

We use limited diagnostic data solely to:

  • Identify and fix crashes and bugs
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Maintain and enhance the user experience

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share limited crash and diagnostic data with trusted third-party service providers (such as Firebase Crashlytics or Sentry) strictly for the purpose of monitoring and improving app performance.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your financial data is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not store or transmit your financial data to any external servers.
  • Crash and diagnostic data transmitted to third-party services is protected using industry-standard encryption in transit.

5. Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.

Crash and diagnostic data collected by third-party services is retained according to their respective policies.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • You can delete your data at any time by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.
  • You can manage device permissions through your device settings.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date or posting a new version of this policy.
