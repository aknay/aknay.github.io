Privacy Policy for TimerThing

Effective Date: June 12, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how the app TimerThing (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) handles user data. By using the app, you agree to this policy.

2. Information We Collect

The app does not require user registration or accounts.

However, we may collect limited data automatically through third-party services:

Device and Usage Data

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App interactions (button taps, timer usage, screen views)
  • App performance data (crashes, errors, diagnostics)
  • Device or advertising identifiers

Advertising Data

  • Advertising ID for ad delivery and measurement

3. How We Use Information

  • To improve app performance, stability, and fix technical issues
  • To analyze usage trends and user behavior to improve the user experience
  • To display and optimize relevant advertisements

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect and process data in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Google AdMob

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data for commercial purposes. Automatically collected data is shared only with the third-party providers listed above for analytics, crash reporting, and advertising purposes.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

TimerThing does not require user accounts and does not store or retain any personal user data on its own servers.

Data Retention Period

Information collected automatically through third-party services (Google Analytics for Firebase, Firebase Crashlytics, and Google AdMob) is retained and processed according to Google's standard retention policies (typically ranging from 14 to 50 months for analytics data, or until anonymized/deleted by the system).

How to Have Your Data Deleted

Because we do not host user accounts or store data on our own servers, there is no direct account data for us to delete. However, users have full control over their data deletion through the following methods:

  • App Uninstallation: You can delete all locally stored app data and stop future data collection immediately by uninstalling TimerThing from your device.
  • Resetting Identifiers: You can delete and reset your device's Advertising ID at any time via your Android device settings (Settings > Privacy > Ads > Reset advertising ID or Delete advertising ID). This decouples past usage data from your device.
  • Manual Deletion Requests: If you wish to request the deletion of any associated analytics or diagnostic data, you can contact us directly at maker.dev.lab@gmail.com. Upon receiving your request, we will initiate deletion requests with our third-party service providers to remove any data associated with your device identifiers to the extent technically feasible.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data, but no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Your Choices & Data Control

  • Disable Personalized Advertising: You can opt-out of personalized ads via your device's privacy settings.
  • Manage/Reset Identifiers: You can limit or clear your advertising identifier through your device's settings.
  • Data Erasure Requests: To request information regarding data retention or to formally request data erasure, please contact us at maker.dev.lab@gmail.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app or on our official privacy policy link.
