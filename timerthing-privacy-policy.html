Privacy Policy for TimerThing

Effective Date: June 12, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how TimerThing (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) handles user data. By using the app, you agree to this policy.

2. Information We Collect

The app does not require user accounts or registration.

We may collect limited data automatically through third-party services used in the app.

Device and Usage Data

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App interactions (such as button taps, timer usage, and screen views)
  • App performance data (crashes, errors, diagnostics)
  • Device or other identifiers (such as Firebase installation ID)

Advertising Data

  • Google Advertising ID (used for ad delivery, frequency capping, and measurement)

3. How We Use Information

  • To improve app performance and stability
  • To understand usage trends and improve user experience
  • To display and measure advertisements
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect and process data:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Google AdMob

These services process data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Some automatically collected data may be shared with third-party service providers for analytics, crash reporting, and advertising purposes.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

TimerThing does not require user accounts and does not store personal data on its own servers.

Data Retention Period

Data collected through Google Analytics for Firebase, Firebase Crashlytics, and Google AdMob is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill analytics and advertising purposes, in accordance with Google's standard retention policies (typically ranging from 14 to 50 months), or until a manual deletion request is processed.

Data Deletion Requests

In compliance with Google Play’s Data Safety and User Data policies, users have full control over the erasure of their data. If you would like to formally request the deletion of analytics, performance, or diagnostic data associated with your device, please contact us directly at maker.dev.lab@gmail.com.

Upon receiving your request, we will verify your device/advertising identifier and initiate a deletion request with our third-party service providers (Google Firebase and Google AdMob) to permanently purge the data from their systems. All manual deletion requests will be fully processed and completed within 30 days.

You may also stop all future data collection and delete locally stored app data immediately by uninstalling the app from your device.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Your Choices & Data Control

  • You may disable personalized ads in your device settings.
  • You may reset or limit your advertising identifier through your device settings.
  • To formally request data erasure across our third-party networks, please email us at maker.dev.lab@gmail.com. Your request will be fulfilled within 30 days.
  • You may uninstall the app at any time to stop data collection.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
