Privacy Policy for timerthing

Effective Date: June 12, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how the app timerthing (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) handles user data. By using the app, you agree to this policy.

2. Information We Collect

The app does not require user registration or accounts.

However, we may collect limited data automatically through third-party services:

Device and Usage Data

  • Device model and operating system
  • App interactions (button taps, timer usage, screen views)
  • App performance data (crashes, errors, diagnostics)
  • Device or advertising identifiers

Advertising Data

  • Advertising ID for ad delivery and measurement

3. How We Use Information

  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Analyze usage and user behavior
  • Display and optimize advertisements
  • Fix bugs and technical issues

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect and process data:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Google AdMob

These services may collect device information, usage data, crash diagnostics, and advertising identifiers in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data for commercial purposes. Some data may be shared with third-party providers for analytics and advertising purposes.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

TimerThing does not require user accounts and does not store personal user data on its own servers.

Information collected through Google Analytics for Firebase, Firebase Crashlytics, and Google AdMob is retained and processed according to Google's applicable policies and retention settings.

Users may stop future data collection by uninstalling the app.

Because we do not maintain user accounts or directly store personal user data, there is generally no account data for us to delete. However, users who have questions about data retention or deletion may contact us at maker.dev.lab@gmail.com, and we will make reasonable efforts to assist with privacy-related requests.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Your Choices

  • You may disable personalized advertising through your device settings where supported.
  • You may reset or limit the use of your device advertising identifier through your device privacy settings.
  • You may uninstall the app at any time to stop future data collection by the app.

For privacy-related questions or requests regarding data retention or deletion, contact maker.dev.lab@gmail.com.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here.
